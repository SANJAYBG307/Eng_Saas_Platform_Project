# Student User Manual

## Getting Started

Welcome to the Engineering SaaS Platform Student Portal. This guide will help you make the most of the system.

## Logging In

1. Go to your college portal
2. Click **Login**
3. Enter your username and password
4. Access your Student Dashboard

## Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- **Upcoming Assignments**: Due soon
- **Recent Grades**: Latest published grades
- **Attendance**: Your attendance percentage
- **Pending Fees**: Outstanding payments
- **Announcements**: Important updates

## Assignments

### Viewing Assignments

1. Click **Assignments** in sidebar
2. See all your assignments
3. Filter by:
   - Subject
   - Status (Pending/Submitted/Graded)
   - Due date

### Submitting Assignments

1. Click on an assignment
2. Read instructions carefully
3. Click **Submit Assignment**
4. Either:
   - Upload file(s)
   - Enter text submission
5. Add optional comments
6. Click **Submit**

**Note**: You can't edit after deadline!

### Checking Grades

1. Go to **Assignments**
2. Click on graded assignment
3. View:
   - Your grade
   - Teacher feedback
   - Class average

## Grades

### Viewing All Grades

1. Click **Grades** in sidebar
2. See grades for all subjects
3. View:
   - Exam type
   - Marks obtained
   - Total marks
   - Percentage
   - Grade

### Grade Statistics

- Subject-wise averages
- Semester GPA
- Rank in class (if available)
- Progress over time

## Attendance

### Checking Attendance

1. Click **Attendance**
2. View your attendance records
3. See:
   - Date
   - Subject
   - Status (Present/Absent/Late/Excused)
   - Total percentage

### Attendance Summary

- Overall attendance percentage
- Subject-wise attendance
- Monthly breakdown
- Warning if below required %

## Timetable

## Fees

### Viewing Fee Details

1. Click **Fees**
2. View all fee items:
   - Tuition
   - Library
   - Lab fees
   - Exam fees
   - Other charges

### Payment History

- See paid fees
- View pending payments
- Download receipts

### Making Payments

1. Click **Pay Fees**
2. Select fee items
3. Click **Proceed to Payment**
4. Choose payment method:
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - Net Banking
   - UPI
5. Complete payment
6. Download receipt

## Announcements

### Viewing Announcements

1. Click **Announcements**
2. Read important updates
3. Announcements include:
   - General notices
   - Exam notifications
   - Event information
   - Holiday notices

### Notifications

- Receive email notifications
- Check regularly for updates

## Communication

### Contacting Teachers

1. Click **Communication**
2. Click **New Message**
3. Select teacher
4. Choose subject
5. Write message
6. Click **Send**

### Message History

- View all your messages
- See teacher replies
- Track conversation threads

## Reports

### Downloading Reports

1. Click **Reports**
2. Select report type:
   - Grade Report
   - Attendance Report
   - Progress Report
3. Choose semester/year
4. Click **Generate**
5. Download PDF

## Profile

### Updating Profile

1. Click your name (top right)
2. Select **Profile**
3. Update:
   - Contact info
   - Profile picture
   - Emergency contacts
4. Click **Save**

### Changing Password

1. Go to Profile
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter current and new password
4. Click **Update**

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Submit Assignment
- Check file size (max 50MB)
- Try different file format
- Check internet connection
- Contact teacher if deadline passed

### Grade Not Showing
- Wait for teacher to publish
- Check in correct subject
- Refresh page
- Contact teacher if delayed

### Payment Failed
- Check card details
- Ensure sufficient balance
- Try different payment method
- Contact accounts department

## FAQs

**Q: Can I submit assignment after deadline?**
A: Contact your teacher directly. Late submissions may have penalties.

**Q: How do I check my class rank?**
A: Go to Grades > Statistics (if enabled by college).

**Q: Can I download all my grades?**
A: Yes, go to Reports > Grade Report > Download.

**Q: What if my attendance is wrong?**
A: Report to teacher within 24 hours of class.

**Q: How do I get fee receipt?**
A: Go to Fees > Payment History > Download Receipt.
